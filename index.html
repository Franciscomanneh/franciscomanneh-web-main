Full Stack Developer | Networking Specialist | Creative Designer

I’m Francisco Manneh, a passionate and ambitious Full Stack Developer with expertise in creating impactful digital solutions. Since 2023, I’ve been helping businesses and individuals establish a strong online presence through modern, functional, and visually appealing websites.

What I Specialize In:

  • Web Development: Building responsive and scalable websites using modern technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Node.js.

  • WordPress Development: Creating professional WordPress websites with customizable themes and plugins.
